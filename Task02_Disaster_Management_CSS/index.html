Founder

Deepti Sree

Building Hands in Need with the belief that technology should reach people when it matters the most.

When disasters strike, help should not depend on money, influence, or familiarity. This platform exists to serve people at their most vulnerable moments, when traditional support systems fall short. Hands in Need is built on a simple belief: no one should be alone when they need help the most.In moments where power, privilege, or relationships offer no relief, this system strives to remain present, responsive, and trustworthy.
