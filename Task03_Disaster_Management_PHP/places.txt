Must visit places before you kick the bucket!!
1 Iguazu Falls (Argentina/Brazil): Massive waterfalls on the border, featuring the "Devil's Throat".
2 Serengeti National Park (Tanzania): Renowned for wildlife and the Great Migration.
3 Great Barrier Reef (Australia): The world's largest coral reef ecosystem.
4 Moraine Lake (Canada): A turquoise glacial lake in Banff National Park.
5 Uluru-Kata Tjuta National Park (Australia): A sacred, massive sandstone monolith. 
6 Taj Mahal (India): An iconic white-marble mausoleum and UNESCO World Heritage site.
7 Machu Picchu (Peru): The "Lost City of the Incas" nestled in the Andes.
8 Rome & The Vatican (Italy): The heart of the Roman Empire and Renaissance art.
9 Angkor Wat (Cambodia): The largest religious monument in the world.
10 Petra (Jordan): An ancient city carved directly into vibrant red rock cliffs.
11 Stonehenge (England): A prehistoric monument shrouded in mystery.
